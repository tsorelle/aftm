Amplify Texas Folklife - March 1

AFTM urges you to support our friends and supporters at Texas Folklife during their on-line fundraising event. Folklife aims to raise $3,000 in 24 hours on March 1.

Go to this Amplify Austin link to contribute: https://amplifyatx.ilivehereigivehere.org/texasfolklife
and forward the link to your friends. And don't be shy to contribute if you miss March 1!

Texas Folklife promotes, presents and documents the state's rich cultural legacy through a vibrant mix of exhibitions, performances, community residencies, apprenticeships, and educational programs around the state. Texas Folklife is committed to preserving the diverse living heritage of Texans, while exploring the vital role of tradition in contemporary society
